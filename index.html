Research研究方向

Generalizable Brain Decoding可泛化脑机解码

We study how to decode visual and semantic information from brain signals when new-subject data are limited and neural responses vary across individuals.

我们面向非侵入式脑信号开展可泛化脑机解码研究，重点探索 fMRI 视觉解码、跨被试迁移、多模态表征对齐与生成式重建，推动脑机接口与多模态人工智能的深度融合。

Cross-subject fMRI visual decoding.跨被试 fMRI 视觉解码

Few-shot adaptation for new subjects.新被试少样本适配

Brain-image-text representation alignment.脑信号、图像与文本表征对齐

Visual retrieval and reconstruction from brain activity.基于脑活动的图像检索与视觉重建
