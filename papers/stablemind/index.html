Abstract摘要

StableMind is a regularized adaptation framework for source-free cross-subject fMRI decoding. It targets a practical deployment scenario in which new-subject fMRI data are limited and raw source-subject data cannot be revisited during adaptation.StableMind 是一个面向源域不可见跨被试 fMRI 解码的正则化适配框架。该方法关注更贴近实际部署的场景：新被试 fMRI 数据有限，并且适配阶段无法重新访问源被试原始数据。

The method stabilizes adaptation from two sides: improving the robustness of brain representations and making image-side supervision more reliable. This design enables more stable brain-image alignment under limited new-subject data.该方法从两个方面稳定适配过程：提升脑侧表征的鲁棒性，并提高图像侧监督的可靠性。通过这一设计，模型能够在有限新被试数据下实现更稳定的脑图像对齐。

Research Background研究背景

Recent fMRI-to-image decoding methods have made strong progress with pretrained vision-language models and generative models. However, many methods still require a large amount of subject-specific fMRI data, which limits their scalability for new users.近年来，预训练视觉语言模型和生成模型推动了 fMRI 到图像解码的发展。然而，许多方法仍然依赖大量受试者专属 fMRI 数据，这限制了其在新用户场景中的可扩展性。

Cross-subject decoding reduces this data burden by pretraining on existing subjects and adapting to a new subject. In practice, however, source-subject data may be inaccessible because of privacy, storage, or data-sharing constraints. This makes source-free new-subject adaptation an important problem.跨被试解码通过在已有被试上预训练、再适配到新被试来降低数据采集成本。但在实际应用中，源被试数据可能因隐私、存储或共享限制而不可访问。因此，源域不可见的新被试适配是一个重要问题。

Research Method研究方法

StableMind contains three complementary modules. Cross-subject ridge reuse introduces source-subject projection priors to regularize the target voxel-to-latent mapping. Feature-level brain augmentation perturbs amplitude statistics in the Fourier domain while preserving phase information, improving robustness to subject-specific feature variations.StableMind 包含三个互补模块。跨被试 ridge 复用利用源被试投影先验来正则化目标被试的体素到潜空间映射。特征级脑表征增强在傅里叶域扰动幅度统计并保留相位信息，从而提升模型对个体特征变化的鲁棒性。

Difficulty-aware image blur further regularizes image-side supervision. Easier samples receive stronger blur to suppress unreliable fine details, while harder samples preserve more visual structure to maintain sufficient alignment signals.难度感知图像模糊进一步正则化图像侧监督。较易对齐的样本施加更强模糊，以抑制不可靠细节；较难对齐的样本保留更多视觉结构，以维持充分的对齐信号。

Experimental Results实验结果

Experiments are conducted on the Natural Scenes Dataset under a unified 1-hour adaptation protocol. StableMind achieves 84.02% image retrieval accuracy and 81.66% brain retrieval accuracy averaged over four target subjects, improving brain retrieval by 5.71% over the strongest compared method.实验在 Natural Scenes Dataset 上采用统一的 1 小时适配协议进行。StableMind 在四个目标被试上的平均图像检索准确率达到 84.02%，脑检索准确率达到 81.66%，相较最强对比方法在脑检索上提升 5.71%。

StableMind also keeps adaptation lightweight, using 4.04M trainable adaptation parameters. Qualitative results show that the reconstructed images better preserve object shapes, colors, and scene layouts compared with previous limited-data cross-subject decoding methods.StableMind 同时保持了较高的适配效率，仅使用 4.04M 个可训练适配参数。定性结果显示，与已有少样本跨被试解码方法相比，StableMind 重建结果在目标形状、颜色和场景布局上更接近原始视觉刺激。
